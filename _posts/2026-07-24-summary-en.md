This work advances algorithmic reasoning and mechanistic interpretability by enabling transformers to execute arbitrary algorithms directly through handcrafted weights, separate from learned behavior, and does so with a standard architecture accessible to the broader community. Torchwright extends these ideas by allowing ordinary Python code as input and targeting a standard, off-the-shelf transformer architecture (Phi-3) compatible with Hugging Face.

World models refer to an agent's internal representation of how the environment evolves over time, and extracting them from video generation models is an active research area for robotics.
